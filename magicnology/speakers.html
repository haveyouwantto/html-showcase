Alice

Princess Alice, the Teen Genius, is a visionary leader in the world of Magicnology. She is widely known as the Mother of Magicnology, credited with inventing groundbreaking enchantments that have brought the benefits of magic to even those without inherent magical abilities.

Alice's most notable invention is the Magic Cloak, a remarkable creation that grants the power of flight to anyone who wears it. Through her collaboration with Sally, she pioneered the development of the first Magic Radio and Magic Computer, ushering in a new era of magical communication and computation.

Sally

Sally, the Logic Enthusiast, is a renowned magictist specializing in the study of magical properties and elemental forces. Her research on the basic properties of elements and the nature of magic has been instrumental in advancing the field of Magicnology.

Sally's collaboration with Princess Alice led to groundbreaking inventions, including the first Magic Radio and the Magic Computer. These inventions revolutionized communication and computation by harnessing the power of magic. Sally's innovative work also extended to the creation of custom magic and the development of the Magic Nexus, a system enabling seamless information and item exchange between towns.
